Industries of the ad/media vendors that Super PACs paid in 2024 (SAM.gov NAICS codes)
These are the industries of firms the Super PACs paid for ad work, classified by their SAM.gov federal-contractor NAICS code. This is the political-advertising supply chain (printing, PR, media buying, digital) not industries donating money.
Note: 616 payments to "META" ($10.3M) were originally mis-matched by SAM.gov to an unrelated social-services contractor ("META LLC", NAICS 624120). These are Meta Platforms digital ad buys and have been reclassified to Software Publishers (digital ads), which is now the #2 vendor industry at $25.4M. See sam_match_review.csv for 51 other matches to verify.
